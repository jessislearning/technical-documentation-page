Scientific paper

A scientific paper is a document detailing the results or progress of scientific research that addresses a specified research problem. When peer-reviewed and published in scientific journals, they are also referred to as ("scientific" or "journal" or "academic") articles, papers, reports, or publications.

Scientific papers are written in technical writing styles, and follow formats that are usually specified by scientific organizations or publishers. The target audience are researchers and organizations that are interested in the continued generation of results, progress, knowledge, innovation, inventions or technologies that emerge from the reported research.
